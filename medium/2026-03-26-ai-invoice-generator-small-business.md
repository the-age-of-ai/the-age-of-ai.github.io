## What Is AI Invoice Generators?
AI invoice generators are software tools that use artificial intelligence to automatically generate professional-looking invoices for small businesses. These tools save time and reduce errors, allowing businesses to focus on what matters most - growing their customer base and increasing revenue.

## Who Should Use This?
• Small business owners with 1-10 employees
• Freelancers or independent contractors who need to send invoices regularly
• Businesses with simple invoicing needs (e.g., services-based industries)
• Companies that want to automate their invoicing process to reduce administrative burden

## Who Should NOT Use This?
• Large corporations with complex invoicing requirements (e.g., multiple departments, products)
• Businesses with extremely irregular or customized invoice formats
• Companies that require human-level customization and negotiation capabilities in invoices
